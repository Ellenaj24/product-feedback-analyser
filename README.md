# Product Feedback Analyser

> A repeatable PM workflow for turning raw customer feedback into prioritised product opportunities, using Qualtrics, Excel and Microsoft Copilot.

🔗 **[View the live case study →](https://[your-username].github.io/[repo-name]/)**

## What this is

A complete product/process design for an internal workflow that:

- Consolidates customer feedback from multiple channels into one source
- Uses Microsoft Copilot to surface themes and pain points at scale
- Scores opportunities against a six-criterion prioritisation framework
- Produces stakeholder-ready outputs that feed directly into the roadmap

## Why this exists

I built this workflow in my role as a Product Manager to solve a real problem: customer feedback was arriving constantly, but the team had no consistent way to synthesise it or prioritise what mattered most. This repo documents the design as a reusable artefact — for my portfolio, for other PMs who might find it useful, and as the kind of evidence-based, repeatable process I believe product teams should be running.
